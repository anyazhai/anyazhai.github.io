User Research

Coaches and athletes are the target group of the product.

User personas were drawn up to understand the user. Major user pain point noticed was the lack of time that both the athletes and coaches had. This pain point was strongly considered throughout the design process.

In response to the user research,

- Color coded scores

- Visual representation of data

were incorporated in the design to enable easy glace through of large amount of data
